Who - me?

Assignment Description

Together with some of your students, prepare a proposal for an exciting, engaging, interesting, amazing presentation of the most important person in your life - you.

The assignment must be done in groups of 5 students.
You must prepare a presentation per. group. This means that the presentation must be the group’s common concept for the presentation of all students in the group.

Groups can choose different structures: There may be a “front page” (depending on selected media) for everyone in the group and then have individual “subpages” or make completely mixed presentation.
You can use a digital or offline media to present the idea.

Your presentation is expected to use design elements:
• Select items
• Images Graphics Fonts Colors
• Organize your items
• Develop layout from concept

The process

The group decided that for this project, we would create a cartoon of ourself by using the bitmoji app.

We all downloaded the app and tried to create the most likely figure of ourself.

Afterward we collected a collage with the bitmoji and other pictures that tells something about one’s self.

Togehter with that we assembled a powerpoint whereas we told different facts about ourself.
