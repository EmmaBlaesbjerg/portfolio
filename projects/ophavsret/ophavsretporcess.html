Assignment Description

Find a well-known image that you obviously do not have copyrights on.

Let’s change that picture.

The process

I started the task of finding a known image that I don’t own the copyright on.

Then I removed the background of the picture, so that only the young couple in the grudge could sees.

Then I found a picture where there is no copyright on, where a person stands and holds a bulb in his hand.

Then I put the kissing couple in the image - as if they were inside the bulb.
