Broer i København

Assignment Description

Look around! Here are bridges, - a lot of bridges.
And each and every one has a story that you’ll find and document with your cameras, whether it is SLR, Lomo or mobile phone.

Take beautiful pictures, ugly pictures, make sketches and drawings, find stones, leaves, vandalism and decay.

Where is your favorite bridge? What is the story?

You can join the pictures yourself or you can not help.

Dokument and show, your gruops day and findings via your picutres.

The process

My gruop startede the task by each finding a picture, we took on our trip into photoshop.

Each of us changede the colour and some removed some elements.

After that we collected them all in a collage.

To conclude we inserted the text to give the image a punctuation and make people stop and fill in the rest of the sentence itself
