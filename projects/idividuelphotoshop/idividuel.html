Idividuel Photoshop

Assignment Description

You must select at least one photo from the series of images taken during the excursion. The image / images do not have to be one of the ones you have taken.

The image / images must be processed in Photoshop so that at least the following are done:

Remove an item: Something on the image must be removed and the background (hole behind) must be filled out to fit the image.

Add an item: Something that was not originally in the picture should be inserted so it appears that it was from the start.

Adjust the image’s lighting conditions or add a mood to the image by filter or color tone Cropping and possibly alignment (vertical / horizontal) of image.

Format and size: The image should be in 4: 3 format and saved as the size is ≦ 1 M pixel

Post the original, unedited image as thumbnail (max 200 pixels for the longest page) in the same record as your edited version.

The process

The image builds on several changes.

The first step I did, was to alter the sky. To do that I found another image of a sky, which had some more colours.
After that did I remove different elements, that seemed out of places.

In addition to the sky, I added other elements - the moon, the flamingos and new people.
Finally, I changed the brightness and format of the imange.
